About Me

I value empathy toward myself and others, as well as a keen understanding of the precise realities that lie beneath individual and organizational stories.

I earned a law degree eight years ago. Afterward I travelled widely and worked, overseas and in Toronto, as a street canvasser, raising over $1M in projected monthly donations for international development charities. In my current role as an in-house fundraiser at a Canadian education charity, I bring in four and five-figure corporate gifts and work collaboratively on galas, memberships and larger corporate grants.

Software solves problems but the best problems, the most interesting ones, also require social instruments like organizations to untangle. Part of my interest in web development and programming stems from understanding that digital transformation helps organizations achieve their goals much more effectively. I also enjoy the technology sector's ambitious learning curve and constant change.
